Projet Name: The Last One 2D Remastered
Platform : Windows & Flash WebGL
Year: 2020



Try
Download for Windows

Description :


TLO2D is an online Battle Royal game whose goal is to be the last survivor on a battlefield against other enemies.


This game was developed by a team of students from EPITECH over a period of 2 years. The project was a success and it can be played on Windows via an Installer or in Flash WebGL even with an inefficient computer configuration.
