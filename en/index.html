  • LOGIC
    Computing requires logic, a bit of basic math and a fairly open mind to abstract concepts.



    AUTONOMY
    You must be able to manage your time and set consistent priorities in order to quickly resolve problems and manage projects on your own.

    ADAPTATION
    This is what makes it possible to understand and evaluate different points of view or to adapt one's approach according to the changes that occur.



    RELATIONAL
    If there is one quality that concerns all the functions of the company, it is the sense of contact. Indeed, it is an ideal quality to work in project mode, to unite a team around you, to go fishing for information.

    ANALYSIS
    It is necessary to have perspective in order to analyze and assess a complex problem. Just cut it out to find simple solutions.

    KNOWLEDGE
    Curiosity and the desire to learn more and more are therefore long-term. At the same time, it is also necessary to question yourself regularly.

    CURIOSITY
    Curiosity and versatility being an asset, do not hesitate to do technological monitoring in the broad sense to be armed in the company. It is necessary to learn about the latest developments, both technical and software.

    CREATIVITY
    The development of a project is not limited to the Back-End but also on the physical and visual aspect of a project. It's not just a talent, it's more a state of mind: imagine to produce workable ideas or invest in reality.
